Symptons of Heart Disease

  1. Extreme Fatigue
  2. Constant dizziness or lightheadness
  3. A fast heart rate(more than 100 beats per minute)
  4. A new, irregular heartbeat
  5. Chest pain or discomfort during activity that goes away with rest
  6. difficulty breathing during regular activities and rest
  7. A respiratory infection or cough that becones worse
  8. Restlessness or confusion
  9. Changes in sleep patterns
  10. Loss of appetite or nausea
