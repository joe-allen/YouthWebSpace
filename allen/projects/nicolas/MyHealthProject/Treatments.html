Treatments of Alzheimer's Disease

Treatments is medical care given to a patient for an illness or injury.
Here are a drug treatments Alzheimer's:


Treatment of general memory loss and confusion:
-Aricept
-Exelon
-Razadyne
-Memantine

Treatment for mood changes and behavioral changes:
-Anti depressants
-citalopram (Celexa)
-fluoxetine (Prozac)
-paroxeine (Paxil)
-sertraline (Zoloft)
-trazodone (Desyrel)

-Anxiolytics (anti-anxiety Medications)
-lorazepam (Ativan)
-oxazepan (Serax)

-Antipsychotics (Medications)
-aripiprazole (Abilify)
-clozapine (Clozaril)
-haloperidol (Haldol)
-olanzapine (Zyprexa)
-quetiapine (Seroquel)
-risperidone (Risperdal)
-ziprasidone (Geodon)
