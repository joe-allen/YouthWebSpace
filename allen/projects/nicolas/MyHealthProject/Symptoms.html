Symptoms of Alzheimer's Disease

Symptoms are a change in the body or mind which indicates that a disease is present.
Here are a few that may cause Alzheimer's:


- Impairment of reasoning and thought
- Poor judgment and decisions
- Unable to plan or perform tasks
- Changes in behavior or personality such as:

Depression
Apathy
Social withdrawal
Mood swings
Distrust in close family and friends
Irritability and aggression
Change in sleeping habits
Wandering
Loss of inhibitions
Delusions
