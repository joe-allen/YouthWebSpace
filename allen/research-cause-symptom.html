Health Research Instructions

Today we are going to research the causes and symptoms for our chosen health topics. What we find will be used next class to build a new page for our Health Website Project.

1. Open a new text document

- You can use Microsoft Word, Workpad, Sublime Text 3, etc. to record your research findings.

- Remember, if you copy information from the Internet, be sure to cite the source (Author / Link Address) from where you got it from.

2. The first page will be the CAUSES of the disease you have chosen to research

- Use the Internet to find information on what causes the disease you have chosen.

- Remember, you will need enough information on the causes of the disease to fill 1 page.

3. The 2nd page will be the SYMPTOMS of the disease you have chosen to research

- Use the Internet to find information on the symptoms of the disease you have chosen.

- Remember, you will need enough information on the symptoms of the disease to fill about 1 page.

4. If you have finished, find a few images on the Internet to support your research

- Again, remember to cite the source (Photographer / Link Address) from where you got it from.
